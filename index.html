Our Vision

To see Black history, culture, and brilliance widely known, proudly celebrated, and uniquely explored through games that bring people together.

Our Mission

I'N'I Games creates engaging board games rooted in Black culture and history, using play as a tool for education, storytelling, and connection across generations and communities.

If you want to be a part of the project, get in touch at alexander.donovan-lowe@inigames.org

Our Mission

At I'N'I Games, our mission is to create innovative and engaging board games that captivate players worldwide.

We strive to tell the histories of Black people in a way that is historically accurate and uplifting.

I'N'I Games will be working to establish a completely sustainable and fair-paying supply chain to bring these games to your tables.

We won't tell stories without including the people they're about in a meaningful way. This means hiring Black artists, illustrators, and game designers
